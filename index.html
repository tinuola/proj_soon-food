Why Soon Food?

Fresh, Seasonal and Tasty

At Soon Food, we believe that food should be fresh and seasonal. We work with only what's in season. We make it colorful, we make it fresh, we make it taste good.

Soon Food Jams are handmade fresh, in small batches, without preservatives, high fructose corn syrup or commercial pectin. All you taste is the fruit and its season.

The Soon Food Concept is available as a private cooking experience for your family and friends. We create custom menus that feature locally sourced, organic ingredients. We bring everything--including pots, dishware and silverware--to you! And clean up after!
